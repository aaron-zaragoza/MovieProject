Welcome to MyMovieList

The site provides its users with a list-like system to organize and score movies and television shows. It facilitates finding users who share similar tastes and provides a large database of movies and television shows, added by community members and admins alike.

Members of the community can discover new movies or shows by filtering by genre, ranking, rating, or more. As new features become available, you will be able to customize and rank your personalized lists of movies to share with friends and add to your personal blog!

We welcome you to the community and hope you enjoy!
